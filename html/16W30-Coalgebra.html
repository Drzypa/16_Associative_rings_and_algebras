coalgebra


A coalgebra is a vector space A over a field 𝕂 with a 𝕂-linear map Δ:A→A⊗A, called the comultiplication, and a (non-zero) 𝕂-linear map ε:A→𝕂, called the counit, such that

(Δ⊗id)∘Δ = (id⊗Δ)∘Δ (coassociativity),
(ε⊗id)∘Δ =id= (id⊗ε)∘Δ.
