finitely generated projective module


Let R be a unital ring. A finitely generated projective right R-module is of the form e⁢Rn, n∈ℕ, where e is an idempotent in EndR(Rn).

Let A be a unital C*-algebra and p be a projection in EndA(An), n∈ℕ. Then, ℰ=p⁢An is a finitely generated projective right A-module. Further, ℰ is a pre-Hilbert A-module with (A-valued) inner product

⟨u,v⟩=∑i=1nui*⁢vi,u,v∈ℰ.
