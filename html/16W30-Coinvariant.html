coinvariant


Let V be a comodule with a right coaction t:V→V⊗A of a coalgebra A. An element v∈V is right coinvariant if

t⁢(v)=v⊗1⁢IA. (1)

The set of coinvariants of A is a sub-comodule with the trivial coaction of A. The sub-comodule of right (or left) coinvariants of V is sometimes denoted by Vco⁢A (or Vco⁢A).
