Let $M$ be a module.  A {\it projective resolution} of $M$
is an exact sequence of the form
$$\cdots \to P_n \to P_{n-1} \to \cdots \to P_1 \to P_0 \to M \to 0$$
where each $P_n$ is a projective module.
