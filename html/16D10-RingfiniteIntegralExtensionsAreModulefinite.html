ring-finite integral extensions are module-finite


Theorem If B is a subring of A and u1,…,us∈A are integral over B, then B⁢[u1,…,us] is module-finite over B.

Proof. If s=1 then un+b1⁢un-1+⋯+bn=0, so {1,u,…,un-1} spans B⁢[u] over B.

If s>1, use induction on B⊂B⁢[u1]⊂B⁢[u1,u2]⊂…⊂B⁢[u1,…,us] and multiply the spanning sets together.
