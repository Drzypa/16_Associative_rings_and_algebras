comodule coalgebra


Let H be a bialgebra. A right H-comodule coalgebra is a coalgebra A which is a right H-comodule satisfying

(Δ⊗id)⁢t⁢(a)=∑a(1)⁢(1)⊗a(2)⁢(1)⊗a(1)⁢(2)⁢a(2)⁢(2),(ε⊗id)⁢t⁢(a)=ε⁢(a)⁢1⁢IH, (1)

for all h∈H and a∈A.

There is a dual notion of a H-module algebra.

Example 1

Let H be a Hopf algebra. Then H is itself a H-comodule coalgebra for the adjoint coaction t⁢(h)=h(2)⊗S⁢(h(1))⁢h(3).
