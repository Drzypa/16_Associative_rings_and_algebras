split short exact sequence


In an abelian category, a short exact sequence 0→A→fB→gC→0 is split if it satisfies the following equivalent conditions:

(a) there exists a homomorphism h:C→B such that g⁢h=1C;

(b) there exists a homomorphism j:B→A such that j⁢f=1A;

(c) B is isomorphic to the direct sum A⊕C.

In this case, we say that h and j are backmaps or splitting backmaps.
