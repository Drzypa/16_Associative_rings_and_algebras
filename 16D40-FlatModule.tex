A right module $M$ over a ring $R$ is {\it flat}
if the tensor product functor $M \otimes_R (-)$ 
is an exact functor.

Similarly, a left module $N$ over $R$ is {\it flat}
if the tensor product functor $(-) \otimes_R N$ 
is an exact functor.
